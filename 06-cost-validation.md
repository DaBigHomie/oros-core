## 1. Strategic Overview

The following cost validations provide a tiered financial roadmap for the Oros platform, scaling from the initial MVP hub in Atlanta to regional dominance and eventually a national enterprise rollout.

**Rate Structure:**
- **Market Rate:** Standard industry pricing for comparable work
- **Strategic Partner Rate:** 80% discount applied for initial growth phases

## 8. Notes & Assumptions

1. **Strategic Partner Rate** is designed to ensure maximum velocity during the MVP cycle
2. Estimates are subject to adjustment based on:
   - Series A participation
   - Technical requirements discovered during development
   - Market conditions
3. All rates assume US-based development standards
4. Third-party costs (Stripe fees, hosting) are pass-through
